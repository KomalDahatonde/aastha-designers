About us

aastha

In the year 2003, aastha was coined when two visionary and wholehearted enthusiasts shared a dream vision of creating better functional and more comfortable surroundings making this world a better place by creating eccentric objects which are “finest” before being sublime.
Our small size makes us approachable to every possible need of the client. From design conceptualization to space planning to interior construction to budgetary concern, we pay attention to each and every microscopic necessity of the client.
Our constant effort lies in making optimum use of resources gathered from man and nature and achieve growth towards excellence and perfection, while contributing to the society and conserving nature with due respect to tradition.
We are providing services in designing interiors as well as architecture compiling residential, retail designs along with exhibition/ small design, adaptive reuse/conversion and refurbishment/restoration.
